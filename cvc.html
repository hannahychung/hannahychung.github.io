The Wheaton College Center for Vocation and Career is an office centered around the mission of equipping students to discover their personal vocations and take steps towards reaching their career goals. As a student-oriented office, the CVC organizes and advertises a wide variety of events to connect students with opportunities and tools in an engaging and professional manner.
