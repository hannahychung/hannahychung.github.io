About

As an illustrator, I enjoy utilizing a whimsical and simple style to capture both the mundane moments of life as well as fantastical dreams and ideas. My work often spans a variety formats, including both digital and physical media, and I often experiment with how my personal style interacts differently in different environments. At its most fundamental, my goal is to make people smile with my illustrations, whether it's because of unexpected placements, pleasing colors, or familiar scenes.
