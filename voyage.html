Voyage Event Series

about

Voyage was the name chosen to encapsulate the Wheaton College Center for Vocation and Career’s semester long Sophomore Series taking place from September through November 2024. This event series consisted of five individual parts, each focused on helping sophomores explore the pieces of their stories and other people’s stories as they figure out how to launch their vocational trajectories.

branding

The goal of Voyage was to appeal to the audience of sophomore college students and to encapsulate the messages of growth and self-discovery. With this in mind, the color palette, font, and logos were designed with a fresh, youthful, and modern brand identity while not straying too much from the CVC’s own branding.
