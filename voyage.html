Voyage

Center for Vocation and Career, Wheaton College

About

Voyage is an event series branding capturing the Wheaton College Center for Vocation and Career's semester-long Sophomore Series. It consists of five sequential events tied together under the theme of embarking on a voyage to better understand one's vocational path.

The goal of Voyage was to appeal to the audience of sophomore college students and to encapsulate the messages of growth and self-discovery. With this in mind, the color palette, font, and logos were designed with a fresh, youthful, and modern brand identity while not straying too much from the CVC's own branding.
